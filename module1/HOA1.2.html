Jollibee Spaghetti: Sweet Style Filipino Spaghetti

Discover Jolly Spaghetti, Jollibee's signature spaghetti dish that offers a delightfully unique take on the classic Italian pasta dish. Bursting with flavor and nostalgia for people with roots in the Philippines, our sweet tomato sauce is balanced by savory notes of garlic, onions, and a hefty dose of ground beef and sliced hotdogs.

Ang Sarap po ng spaghetti, ang tamis!

- Engr. Vince, Jollibee Spaghetti Enthusiast

Ingredients:

  • Oil: 2 spoons
  • Garlic: 1 bulb
  • Onion: 2 pcs
  • Ground Pork: 500g
  • Cheese: 300g, grated
  • Sliced Hotdogs: 250g
  • Fiesta Spaghetti Pack: 1kg

Preparation:

  1. Slice the garlic, onions, and hotdogs.
  2. Heat up oil in the pan and add garlic and onions.
  3. Once fragrant, add ground pork and incorporate it with onion and garlic.
  4. Once cooked, add hotdogs and boil 2kg of water from an another container.
  5. Add the sauce to the pan and let it simmer while boil the noodles in the other container for 10 minutes.
  6. Once everything is cooked, strain the water from the noodles and mix until well combine with the sauce.

Cooking Tips:

  • Browning the ground pork adds more flavor.
  • You may add red bell pepper to be stir fried together with garlic and onions for flavor.
